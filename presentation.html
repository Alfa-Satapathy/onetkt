Slide 8: UX & Design Principles

  • User Experience (UX) Goals:
    • Simplicity: Ensure ease of use for bus operators, owners, and drivers, with a minimal learning curve.
    • Real-Time Data: Focus on delivering real-time updates on fleet health, location, ticket sales, and maintenance schedules.
    • Customizability: Different operators have different needs; make the platform modular and customizable.
    • Mobile-First Approach: Build a responsive mobile app for owners and drivers to manage the fleet remotely.
    • Clear and Visual Reporting: Use visual dashboards (graphs, charts) to help operators understand operational and financial data at a glance.
